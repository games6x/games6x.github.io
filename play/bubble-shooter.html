What is Bubble Shooter?

Bubble Shooter is a classic match-3 puzzle game where you shoot colored bubbles to create matches! This addictive unblocked puzzle game challenges your aim and strategic thinking. Perfect for quick gaming sessions at school or work.

How to Play Bubble Shooter Online

Use your mouse to aim and shoot bubbles! Match three or more bubbles of the same color to make them pop. Clear all the bubbles from the screen to advance to the next level. Each level presents new challenges and bubble arrangements.

Game Controls:

  • Mouse: Aim and shoot bubbles
  • Left Click: Shoot bubble
  • Right Click: Alternative actions
  • Space: Pause/Resume game
  • R: Restart current level
  • H: Show/hide hints
  • M: Toggle music/sound

Tips to Master Bubble Shooter

  1. Plan Your Shots: Look for the best angles to create matches
  2. Use Bank Shots: Bounce bubbles off walls to reach difficult spots
  3. Clear from Bottom: Start clearing bubbles from the bottom up
  4. Look for Chains: Set up chain reactions for big scores

  6. Stay Patient: Take your time to aim accurately
