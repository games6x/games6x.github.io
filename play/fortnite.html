What is Fortnite?

Fortnite is the ultimate battle royale experience where 100 players fight to be the last one standing. Build structures, collect weapons, and outlast your opponents in this fast-paced unblocked shooting game. Perfect for competitive players looking for intense multiplayer action at school or work.

How to Play Fortnite Online

Drop onto the island, scavenge for weapons and materials, build defensive structures, and eliminate other players while staying within the shrinking storm circle. The last player or team standing wins!

Game Controls:

  • WASD: Move your character
  • Mouse: Look around and aim
  • Left Click: Shoot/Attack
  • Right Click: Aim down sights
  • Q, E, R, T, Y: Switch weapons
  • F: Interact/Pick up items
  • G: Throw grenades
  • Space: Jump
  • Shift: Sprint
  • Ctrl: Crouch
  • Tab: Open inventory
  • M: Open map
  • 1-5: Building materials
  • Mouse Wheel: Switch between building pieces

Features & Benefits

  • Battle Royale Mode: 100-player elimination matches
  • Building System: Construct walls, ramps, and structures for defense
  • Weapon Variety: Assault rifles, shotguns, sniper rifles, and more
  • Storm Mechanics: Shrinking play area keeps matches intense
  • Unblocked Access: Play at school and work with no downloads
  • Cross-Platform: Play with friends on different devices

Tips to Win in Fortnite

  1. Land Strategically: Choose less crowded areas to gear up safely
  2. Master Building: Quick building can save your life in combat
  3. Manage Resources: Balance materials, ammo, and healing items
  4. Stay in the Circle: Always be aware of the storm's position
  5. Use Sound: Listen for footsteps and gunshots to locate enemies
  6. High Ground Advantage: Building up gives you better positioning
